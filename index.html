Roadmap

v1.0 is offline-first. Your data stays on your device unless you export or restore a backup.

v1.1.x updates will focus on small improvements, fixes, polish, and quality-of-life changes over the next 3-6 months.

v2.0 is planned to add optional cloud sync, while keeping offline use available.

Danger Zone

Permanently reset all local app data on this device: sessions, tasks, exams, chapters, subjects, settings, and stored backups. Export a backup first.
